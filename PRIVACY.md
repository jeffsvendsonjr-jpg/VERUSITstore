# Privacy Policy for VerusIT

**Effective Date:** January 2025

## Overview

VerusIT is a browser extension that helps users identify the age and relevance of Google search results. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## Data Collection

**VerusIT does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

### What We Don't Collect
- No browsing history
- No search queries
- No personal information
- No analytics or telemetry
- No cookies or tracking

### Local Processing Only

All VerusIT functionality operates entirely within your browser:

1. **Date Detection** - Analyzes search result snippets locally to extract publication dates
2. **Tech Debt Scanning** - Pattern matching runs locally in your browser

## Permissions Explained

### https://www.google.com/*

This permission allows VerusIT to inject freshness badges into Google search results. The extension only runs on Google search pages and does not access any other websites.

## Third-Party Services

VerusIT does not integrate with any third-party services, APIs, or analytics platforms.

## Data Storage

VerusIT does not use browser storage (localStorage, IndexedDB, or chrome.storage) to persist any data between sessions.

## Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the effective date at the top of this document.
